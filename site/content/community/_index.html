Community

Do you want to help build Sesame?

If you’re a newcomer, check out the Good first issue and Help wanted labels in the Sesame repository.

Join the Sesame Office Hours, a place where you can ask questions like “how do I do this in Sesame” and “why does Sesame do this thing this way?”

Join our Kubernetes Slack channel and chat with over 500 other community members: #Sesame

Get updates on Twitter at @projectsesame

Join the Project Sesame Users mailing list for Sesame news, release notifications, invitations to the community meetings, to discuss issues, or to provide suggestions and feedback.

Join the Sesame Community Meetings on Zoom:

  • For Australia time zones:
    • Every first and third Tuesday at 6:30 PM Eastern Time / 3:30 PM Pacific Time / Wednesday at 9:30 AM Australian Eastern Time
  • For Americas time zones:
    • Every second and fourth Tuesday at 1 PM Eastern Time / 10 AM Pacific Time
  • Join the mailing list to get an automated invitation to the meeting
